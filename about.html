About

QADOWSH ADERAYO

ASoul Singer

Qadowsh is a soul singer who blends contemporary and classical music to tell her audience the beautiful story of God, life and nature.
Born in Lagos, Qadowsh spent her younger years experiencing the northern and western culture because of her travelling father whose job took him from city to city. She eventually moved from Gombe to Lagos where she finished her secondary school education.

Her musical journey started for her in secondary school, where she had to lead the school's choir which she did beautifully and even got an award recognising her as ‘’Most Gifted Singer’’ in the school. This quest further led her to join a singing group called Lifestyl in Somolu. With Lifestyl, she explored music and singing more which gave her the sure footing she needed professionally.
Her love and passion for music even became much stronger that she gradually ditched her initial desire to study Occupational Therapy in the university which would have pleased her father.
She enrolled in a number of singing contests to show her singing prowess and made friends who are now noted industry people along the way.

Education

Musical Education and Certification

Qadowsh had to enrol for another JAMB exam to choose Music as her course of Study. She successfully got an admission to study Music in University of Lagos and graduated in 2014.
Following that, Qadowsh won an MTN Scholarship to bag a Diploma in Music in MUSON which she successfully did in 2017 and got an award also as Best Vocalist of her class.
As she was studying in both institutions, Qadowsh sat for the Associated Board of Royal School of Music (ABRSM) Grades seven and eight (Music Theory and practical) and passed colourfully.
Qadowsh has also tested her hands on a couple of musical instruments like the piano, trumpet and violin. Till date, she seizes every opportunity at her disposal to sharpen her skill and music knowledge.

Works

HISTORY OF MUSICAL WORKS

Works

Before she enrolled in university, Qadowsh also known as Qadowsh has been singing professionally and working as recording artiste and background vocalist with leading singers like Kenny K’ore and Lara George and a number of artistes.

Qadowsh has officially released three songs to her credit, one of which is her musical work in MUSON titled Olusomi, a beautiful classical piece in Yoruba. She also organises a yearly worship event tagged ‘’Worship Unleashed’’ which convenes believers to worship in one accord.

She has played the following roles in various musical works and operas:

  • Doubled as chorus member, costume, makeup and stage manager in Patience by Arthur Sullivan 2016.
  • Also in 2016, she was Assistant Director in Don Pasquale By Gaetano Donizetti organized by The Comic Opera house.
  • Featured in Magic Flute by Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart with Muson students as the second lady in the “Three Ladies” role in 2017.
  • Wore many caps as costume manager, stage manager and makeup artist in La Fille du Régiment in 2017. It was a beautiful outing that got resounding accolades and had to be repeated in 2018 also.
  • Worked as makeup and costumes manager in La Bohême by Giacomo Puccini which was a MUSON collaboration with the Switzerland Consulate in 2018.
  • Makeup artist on Don Pasquale crew in 2019.

Qadowsh’s quest for creating musical and singing enthusiasm in people has led her to work as voice trainer and music teacher with Vocal Plus Academy and Brand Points Consults where she contributes her knowledge of music.
