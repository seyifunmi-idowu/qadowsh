ABOUT

I'M QADOWSH ADERAYO

Qadowsh is a soul singer who blends contemporary and classical music to tell her audience the beautiful story of God, life and nature. Born in Lagos, Qadowsh spent her younger years experiencing the northern and western culture because of her travelling father whose job took him from city to city. She eventually moved from Gombe to Lagos where she finished her secondary school education.
